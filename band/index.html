            The Band

            Hearts & Bones


            Musicians

            Richard Strick

            Writer, singer, and performer - has a long history on the bay area music scene. His own solo music is available on CDbaby and at performances.

            Dennis Mahoney

            Drummer - drives the rhythm using his strong sense of percussive accents. Now he chairs Hearts & Bones and keeps the energy up.

            Kent Hayden

            Guitar, keyboards - has played in rock, jazz and big band settings. Always striving for the Dynamics that Hearts and Bones puts out, he contributes to the sound that makes it unique.

            Poncho Paradise

            Writer, singer, and performer- is a longstanding solo act playing around San Fransisco and now in the Lake and Sonoma County. Poncho's song can be sampled at iTunes under his band names, WITHOUT MIRRORS or PONCHO IN PARADISE.
